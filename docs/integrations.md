# Integrations

## Overview

The Integrations page allows you to connect UptimeRobot with various third-party services to receive notifications and alerts when your monitors go down or come back online. This ensures your team stays informed about service availability in real-time.

## URL

`/integrations` - Main integrations dashboard

## Available Integrations

### Chat Platforms

#### Slack
- **Description**: Send monitor status notifications directly to Slack channels
- **Use Case**: Inform your entire team of downtime incidents
- **Availability**: Solo, Team, and Enterprise plans
- **Setup**: Click "Upgrade now" to access Slack integration

#### Telegram
- **Description**: Receive telegram messages for monitor alerts
- **Use Case**: Stay alerted on your mobile device via Telegram
- **Availability**: Solo, Team, and Enterprise plans
- **Setup**: Click "Upgrade now" to access Telegram integration

#### Discord
- **Description**: Get monitor status updates in your Discord server
- **Use Case**: Notify gaming communities or development teams
- **Availability**: Available for setup
- **Setup**: Click "Add" button to configure Discord integration

#### Mattermost
- **Description**: Receive status updates in your Mattermost workspace
- **Use Case**: Enterprise team communication and alerts
- **Availability**: Solo, Team, and Enterprise plans
- **Setup**: Click "Upgrade now" to access Mattermost integration

#### MS Teams
- **Description**: Get notifications in Microsoft Teams channels
- **Use Case**: Corporate environments using Microsoft ecosystem
- **Availability**: Solo, Team, and Enterprise plans
- **Setup**: Click "Upgrade now" to access MS Teams integration

#### Google Chat
- **Description**: Receive notifications in Google Chat spaces
- **Use Case**: Teams using Google Workspace
- **Availability**: Available for setup
- **Setup**: Click "Add" button to configure Google Chat integration

### Technical Integrations

#### Webhook
- **Description**: Set up custom webhooks for advanced alerting
- **Use Case**: Connect to your own systems or third-party applications
- **Availability**: Team and Enterprise plans
- **Setup**: Click "Upgrade now" to access webhook functionality

## Integration Categories

The integrations are organized into several categories:

1. **All** - View all available integrations
2. **Chat platforms** - Communication tools like Slack, Discord, etc.
3. **Webhooks** - Custom HTTP endpoints for advanced integrations
4. **Connectors & Incident management** - Third-party incident management tools
5. **Push notifications** - Mobile and desktop notification services
6. **API** - Programmatic access to UptimeRobot data

## Setting Up Integrations

### General Steps

1. Navigate to the Integrations page (`/integrations`)
2. Use the search bar to find specific integrations: "Search by integration type..."
3. Choose your desired integration from the list
4. Follow the platform-specific setup instructions
5. Configure notification preferences and monitor assignments

### Plan Requirements

- **Free Plan**: Limited integration options
- **Solo/Team Plans**: Access to most chat platform integrations
- **Enterprise Plan**: Full access to all integrations including advanced webhooks

## Managing Integrations

- **Search**: Use the search functionality to quickly find specific integration types
- **Categories**: Filter integrations by category using the left sidebar
- **Status**: View which integrations are already configured vs. available
- **Upgrades**: Many integrations require plan upgrades - use "Upgrade now" links to access additional features

## Notes

- Integration availability varies by subscription plan
- Some integrations may require additional authentication with third-party services
- Webhook integrations provide the most flexibility for custom notification workflows
- Multiple integrations can be configured simultaneously for redundant alerting
